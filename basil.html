About Basil

Basil is the friend of Sunny, and is a prominent character within the OMORI game. He is shy and very soft-spoken, but cares deeply for Sunny and enjoys spending time with him and their friends. Basil is interested in gardening and cultivating flowers, and when they were younger he was interested in photography, taking many pictures of Sunny and Co. throughout their younger years.
